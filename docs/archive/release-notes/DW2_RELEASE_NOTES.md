Date: 2026-07-17

## Scope

DW2 expands the household dishwasher cluster from 15 to 29 pages. It adds AquaStop and continuous-drain scenarios, detergent and dispenser diagnostics, spray-arm and residue pages, foam/noise/door scenarios, and service pages for the inlet valve, door lock, dispenser, level sensor, and control board.

## Safety and diagnostic boundaries

- AquaStop and door safety interlocks must not be bypassed.
- A tablet that does not dissolve is not automatically a dispenser failure.
- White residue is not automatically a pump or salt-system failure.
- Noise must be classified by cycle stage before a pump is replaced.
- Board repair requires diagnosis of connected sensors and loads.
- Excessive foam can cause leakage and must be treated as a stop-use condition when water escapes the cabinet.
