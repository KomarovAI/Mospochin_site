## Publication rule

No page is published from a title list alone. Before publication it needs official evidence, a unique diagnostic tree, metrics context, builder source, crawlable links, canonical/sitemap parity, forms and manual visual review.

## Subclusters

1. Storage refrigeration and cold rooms.
2. Blast chillers and shock-freezing rooms.
3. Ice machines, separated into cube/crescent and auger-based flake/nugget families.

## Forbidden shortcuts

- universal refrigerant pressures;
- “just refill refrigerant” diagnosis;
- appliance error code without model/controller scope;
- mixing storage and blast-chilling intent;
- applying auger faults to cube machines;
- identifying brand/model from a generic photo.
